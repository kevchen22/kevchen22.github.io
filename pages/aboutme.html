Adventures & About Me!

Here are pictures of some recent adventures I have been on:

Blue Hills Reservation in Milford, MA.

I had the luxury of taking some time off in between my work and when I start my class at Per Scholas. On a beautiful sunnny day, I decided to go on a solo hike (my second hike ever). It was one of the most satisfying and rewarding experiences I have been on and now I have a new hobby!

Hiking at Mount Washington with my college roommates.

This was the first hike I have ever been on. In late September of 2019 my college roommates and I decided to go camping (my first time camping as well). It was a challenge to get to the top but I'm glad we stuck it out until the end.

Spring Break in 2018 at Daytona Beach

In my Junior year of college, my roommates and I went to Daytona Beach for spring break. It was also coincidentally during bike week which we didn't know about, so it was a plus to see all these cool motorcycles around town (except for when we were trying to sleep, no need for an alarm clock)! We drove all the way down for the trip (22 hour drive one way), but it was definitely worth it.
